Frequently Asked Questions

What is the motto of MDIS?

EDUCATION IS LIGHT. Here at MDIS, we are big on Education and believe that Education is one of the ways we can empower the teeming population of rising youths. MDIS is a camp where giants and pathfinders are raised.

What are your core values at MDIS?

Godliness, Intergity, Peaceful coexistence, Grit and Hardwork.

As a parent/guardian, can I pay fees in installment?

The answer is, YES! you can pay the fees of your children/ward with up to three installments, we believe that every child deserves a glorious future through out top-notch education.

What year did MDIS fully begin his academic activities?

September 12, 2022.

Do you offer financial aid/scholarships?

No for now. We hope to offer financial aid/scholarships very soon.

Is MDIS a tech compliant school?

Of course!! We are very big on tech at MDIS, as such we pride ourselves as being the sillicon valley of Nigeria.

Why should I choose MDIS?

MDIS has a unique approach to education. With our expanded British curriculum, serene environment, hands-on experience while learning and our, "learn at your pace" mantra, there is no better place for your child.

Is MDIS located in a safe environment?

YES! MDIS is situated in an open environment that steals the attention of the public sometimes. LOL

With the way the world is going today, are my children/ward's future secure?

Yes it is! At MDIS, we are duty-bound to see that your child thrives academically, spiritullay and intellectually. Though our STEM courses, expanded british curriculum, clubs and societies and non-fiction library, your child will definitely graduate as a paragon of success.

What inspired the name, Matthew Danjuma International School?

MDIS was named after Late Matthew Danjuma Oguche. The young Matthew was a rising star. Being the second son of his father, he brought great honour to the Oguche's family though his unwillingness to give up easily. Matthew's life was a testament to the fact that God is still in the business of blessing people till this present time and age. He graduated from Landmark University and went on to achieve remarkable results. Indeed, he was a pathfinder, a pacesetter and a wonder to his world.
